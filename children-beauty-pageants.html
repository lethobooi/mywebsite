Welcome my lil care bears  

TODAY we are unvailing a dark side of glamour 

The recent "Little Miss Sunshine'' beauty pageant , held on August 25 2024, in Los Angeles has sparked a heated debate about the ethics of child protection in such events. The competion which featured over 50 contestants aged 4-12 , was won by a 6 year old , who was crowned the ''Ultimate Litlle Miss Sunshine ''.

Among the contestants , a 9 year old stood out with her talent and charisma. however , her mom, expressed concern about the pressure and emphasis on physical appearance. Another contestant aged 11 has been participating i n pageants for 3 years,but her mother admitted that her daughters focus on appearance is affecting her grades. 

'' Sometimes I feel like Im losing myself in all of this. I just want to be a kid again,'' Sophia.P , Contestant  . '' These children are learning valuable skills, but we do need to ensure we're not cacrificing their childhood,''- Rachel Lee coach 

This event highlighted the need for stricter regulations to protect children from explotation. As the debate rages on, its clear that the glamour of child beauty pageants hides a darker reality. The children's stories serve as a reminder that their well-being and protection should be top priority. The pageant world must evolve to prioritize their needs, ensuring that childhood is preserved and not lost in the pursuit of trophies and titles.
